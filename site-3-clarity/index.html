Fair questions

Asked by every business we meet.

Is my business too small for this? +
No — and you're the reason we exist. 80% of UK businesses haven't adopted AI meaningfully, mostly because everything on offer was built for enterprises. Our smallest engagements automate one job (the inbox, the reports, the follow-ups) and prove themselves in days.
What happens to our data? +
Where your data is sensitive, the AI runs on your infrastructure — it never leaves your network. 53% of organisations name data privacy as their top AI blocker; we think that's a reason to own your AI rather than rent it from a platform.
We tried ChatGPT. It didn't do much. +
A tool isn't a strategy. Chat sessions help one person, once. We build systems — monitoring, generating, escalating — that run daily without anyone prompting them. That's the difference between dabbling and capability.
What does it cost? +
Our pricing principle is simple: the first engagement should cost less than the value it delivers. We scope that together in the audit — and if the numbers don't work, we'll tell you before you spend anything.
Are we locked in once you build it? +
The opposite. Code, configuration, documentation and training all transfer to you. When we leave, the capability stays. You renew because it works, not because you're stuck.
